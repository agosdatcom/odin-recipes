Grilled Cheese Recipe

Enjoy this classic and simple grilled cheese sandwich!

About This Recipe

This grilled cheese recipe is a timeless favorite, perfect for a quick meal or snack. It's easy to make and can be customized with your favorite cheeses and add-ins.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (your choice)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your favorite)
  • Butter or margarine
  • Optional: tomato slices, bacon, or avocado for extra flavor

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the skillet.
  4. Add the cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Carefully flip the sandwich and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Remove from skillet, slice in half, and serve hot!
