S'mores Recipe

Enjoy the classic campfire treat with this simple recipe!

Ingredients

  • Graham crackers
  • Chocolate bars
  • Marshmallows

Instructions

  1. Roast marshmallows over a fire until golden brown.
  2. Place a piece of chocolate on a graham cracker.
  3. Add the roasted marshmallow on top of the chocolate.
  4. Top with another graham cracker and press gently.
  5. Enjoy your delicious s'more!
